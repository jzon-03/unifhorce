About Unifhorce

Bridging Filipino healthcare excellence with American opportunities

Our Mission

Founded in 2024 and based in Rochester, New York, Unifhorce is a newly established nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering Filipino healthcare professionals in their transition to practice in the United States. As a growing organization, we provide comprehensive support from credential evaluation to career advancement, ensuring that talented Filipino healthcare workers can contribute their skills and expertise to American healthcare systems.

Why Filipino Healthcare Workers?

  • Philippines is one of the world's largest exporters of healthcare professionals
  • Filipino nurses and healthcare workers are known for their compassion, dedication, and clinical excellence
  • Strong English proficiency and cultural adaptability
  • Commitment to patient care and professional development
